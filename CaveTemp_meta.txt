# cavetemp.csv
# data simulated (with mult.rnorm) from results in table 1 of Raesly, R. and E. Gates.
#   1987.  Winter habitat selection by north temperate cave bats.  American Midland
#   Naturalist, 118:15-31.
# The ambient temperature was measured on limestone caves in Maryland, Pennsylvania,
#   and West Virginia inhabited by five difference species of bats.  Temperatures were
#   measured between 15Nov and 31Mar.
# amb.temp: Ambient temperature of cave (Celsius)
# species: Species of bat inhabiting the cave.
