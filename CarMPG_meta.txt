# CarMPG.csv  (tab-delimited)
# Industry-reported summaries for the make of a car.
#   mpg = Miles per gallon of gasoline for the car.
#   weight = Weight of car in pounds.
#   drat = Drive-ratio of car.
#   hp = Horsepower of the car engine.
#   disp = Displacement of the car engine in cubic inches.
#   type = Type of origin of the car: Domestic is built in U.S.,
#             Import is built in Europe or Japan
#   cyl = Cylinders of the car engine.  Only three choices (four, six, or eight),
#             so consider this to be categorical.
