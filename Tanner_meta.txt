# Tanner.csv
# Data read from Table 1 in Zhou, S. and G.H. Kruse.  2000.  Modifications of cod pots to reduce tanner crab bycatch.  North American Journal of Fisheries Management 20:897-907.
# trial: trial number
# type: type of cod pot variation -- standard, slick.board, ZK.excluder, and cod.sock.
# approach: number of tanner crabs that approached cod pots.
# reached: number of tanner crabs that reached (insereted any body part into pot opening) the cod pots.
# entered: number of tanner crabs that actually entered the trap completely.
# authors compared reach probability (reached/approached) and entry probability (entered/reached)
#  among four cod pot variation types.
